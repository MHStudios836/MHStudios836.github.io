OPERATIONAL WORKFLOW

FROM CONCEPT TO DEPLOYMENT

PHASE 1: ORGANIZATION

Fully operational collection activities. The Creative Arts® team prepares and gathers creative resources to maintain project assets. Following this, MH Designs™ initiates a brainstorming operation to decide the final distribution of any design under development.

PHASE 2: OPERATION INITIATION

The "Horsemen Team" takes command. Known for effective performance, they ascend the project from organization to deployment, tracking the scope until at least 70% of the mission objectives are complete.

PHASE 3: DEPLOYMENT & DEBUGGING

The Execution Team carries out the final 30%. Their mission is to review final levels, eliminate bugs, optimize performance, and deploy the solution according to the planned scope.
